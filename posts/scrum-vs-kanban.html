About This Site

Why this exists, what I believe, and how to read it.

I’m not here to give you financial advice — I’m not regulated, and I don’t want to be. I built this site because the more I learned about the financial world, the more I needed to make sense of it. Writing helps me map relationships, connect concepts, and see the bigger picture.

Why Individuals Often Have an Edge

After more than a decade in financial services, I’ve learned something that isn’t obvious: individuals can have a structural advantage over professionals. In high-street banks and large institutions, incentives and regulation shape what can be offered. Product shelves reflect compliance and self-interest as much as what’s best for clients. Specialists get pigeonholed into narrow lanes and miss entire corners of the market.

Crypto is a good example. Big institutions often avoid talking about it — not because they don’t understand it, but because it’s volatile, largely unregulated, and awkward to fit into their model. The silence can look out-of-touch to younger investors. The result: it’s hard to get a clear picture from any single source.

What This Site Is (and Isn’t)

Important: This site is not financial advice. I’m not FCA-regulated, and I don’t intend to be.

I share how I invest, the tools I use, and the principles that guide me — not to tell you what to do, but to cut through noise so you can think for yourself. Everyone is involved in financial markets whether they like it or not; the goal here is to make sense of what’s happening, plainly and transparently.

Operating Principles

  • I might be wrong. Change your mind when the evidence changes.
  • No advice. I don’t give financial advice. Ever.
  • Keep digging. Stay curious. Trace claims to first principles and data.

Why This Matters Now

The truth is, nobody will ever care more about your money than you. With inflation quietly eroding savings and state pensions under strain, investing is no longer optional.

Good news: it’s never been easier or cheaper to start.
Bad news: it’s never been more important.
